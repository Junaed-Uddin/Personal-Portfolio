Hello! I'm Junaed Uddin, a former Associate Programmer based in IT Bangla Ltd. I hold a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science & Engineering from Ahsanullah University of Science & Technology in January 2021.

I have maintained, developed and launched multiple projects from scratch, carrying the development of its' back-end and front-end codebases.
